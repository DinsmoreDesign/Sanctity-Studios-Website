your sound

Redefined

∎

Sanctity Studios is a professional recording studio located in north east Mesa, Arizona. We specialize in metal, hard rock and other heavier genres, but are more than willing to take on any project from blues to jazz, pop to rock.

Studio time can be pricey. We work with your budget to deliver the highest quality audio tracks at an affordable, competitive and attractive price!

Serving the Valley, we retain a relaxed atmosphere to let your creativity bloom. Offering a friendly and affordable recording solutions, Sanctity Studios is here to offer reasonable rates, with great results!

what we can do

For You

∎
01.

Tracking

Set in a residential neighborhood, our facilities are both homey and spacious. Our live room has been finely tuned to offer excellent acoustics for drums, guitars and other acoustic instruments.

02.

Editing

We take pride in our work and want you to sound as best as you can! Offering editing services, we're able to tighten up your performances without making you sound like a robot (well... unless you want to).

03.

Mixing

This is where the magic happens! We'll take your raw tracks, smash 'em together and make 'em sound good (trust me, it's a little more complicated than that!). Reamping services available!

04.

Mastering

This is where we take a good mix and make it even better. We'll get your single or album ready for distribution, make sure it meets loudness standards and sweeten the sound so fans will want to crank it to 11!
